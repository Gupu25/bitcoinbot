# Proyecto B.O.B.: Bitcoin Operated Brain

Pitch: Bitcoin Agent — Educational tool for the Mexican audience
Duration: ~90 seconds

### Opening

Most Bitcoin content is in English, technical, and boring. In Mexico we receive more remittances than almost any country in the world, yet very few people understand why Bitcoin matters for them. We built Bitcoin Agent so anyone can understand Bitcoin in Spanish, at their own pace, without signing up or being tracked.

### The Problem

Mexicans have strong reasons to use Bitcoin—remittances, inflation, limited access to banks—but education is the main barrier. Existing tools are either too basic or too technical. People get confused and stop learning.

### Our Solution

Bitcoin Agent is an educational platform built specifically for a Mexican audience. It includes:

B.O.B. — “Bitcoin Operated Brain” — an AI tutor that answers anything in Spanish or English, in simple language. You can ask “¿Cómo compro mi primer Bitcoin?” or “¿Cómo funciona la minería?” and get clear explanations, backed by curated Bitcoin knowledge.

### Why Bitcoin for Mexico — A section focused on real Mexican use cases: cheaper remittances, protection from inflation, financial inclusion, and self-custody.

Interactive labs — Not just text. Users practice: Seed Phrase Lab (your keys, your coins), Merkle Tree Lab (how Bitcoin verifies blocks), Mining Simulator, Signing Lab, and even a Taxes Lab for Mexican tax rules.

Practical path — From learning to buying: we connect users with Aureo, a Mexican platform that supports SPEI, Lightning, and fair fees.

### Why It Works

100% in Spanish — No more translating or guessing.
Learn by doing — Labs, quizzes, and simulations instead of walls of text.
No signup — Open the page and start learning.
No tracking — Education first, no dark patterns.
Closing

Bitcoin Agent is the friendly guide we wish we had when we started. It’s built for Mexicans, by Mexicans, to turn “¿qué es Bitcoin?” into “ahora entiendo cómo puedo usarlo.”

Gracias.
